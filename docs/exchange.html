How does the IOTAI P2P Exchange work?

Trade your IOTAI tokens for USDT (Tether) on the Tron network (TRC-20) in a secure, peer-to-peer way with escrow protection.

S If you want to SELL IOTAI

  • Step 1: Login with your IOTAI wallet (seed phrase)
  • Step 2: Register your Tron wallet address where you will receive USDT
  • Step 3: Go to "Sell IOTAI" tab, set the amount and price per IOTAI
  • Step 4: Your IOTAI is locked in escrow automatically (safe and secure)
  • Step 5: Wait for a buyer. When they pay, the system verifies the transaction automatically
  • Step 6: If auto-verification fails, you can manually confirm that you received the USDT
  • Step 7: Once confirmed, the IOTAI is released to the buyer. Done!

B If you want to BUY IOTAI

  • Step 1: Login with your IOTAI wallet (seed phrase)
  • Step 2: Register your Tron wallet address (where you send USDT from)
  • Step 3: Browse the "Market" tab and find an order you like
  • Step 4: Click "Buy" — the system gives you payment instructions: amount, destination wallet, and a memo code
  • Step 5: Send the USDT to the seller's Tron address (you have 2 hours to complete)
  • Step 6: After sending, paste the Tron transaction hash (TX ID) and confirm
  • Step 7: The system verifies your payment on the Tron blockchain and releases your IOTAI instantly!

You CAN send USDT from any exchange or wallet!

It doesn't matter if you send the USDT from a centralized exchange like Binance, KuCoin, Bybit, OKX, or any other platform. Our system does NOT verify the sender address — it only verifies that the correct amount arrived at the seller's wallet on the Tron blockchain.

This means you can withdraw USDT from your exchange account directly to the seller's address. Just make sure you select the TRC-20 network when withdrawing.

What is escrow and how does it protect me?
When a seller creates an order, their IOTAI tokens are locked in an escrow address on the IOTAI DAG. This means the seller cannot spend those tokens while the order is active. The IOTAI is only released to the buyer after the USDT payment is verified, or returned to the seller if the order is cancelled or expires. This protects both parties.
How is the USDT payment verified?
When the buyer submits their Tron TX hash, our system queries the TronGrid API to verify the transaction on the Tron blockchain. It checks that:
• The transaction was successful
• It involves the official USDT (TRC-20) contract
• The destination address matches the seller's registered Tron wallet
• The amount is correct (with 1% tolerance for network fees)

If automatic verification fails for any reason, the order goes to "pending verification" status and the seller can manually confirm that they received the USDT.
Why do I need a Tron wallet address?
USDT (Tether) runs on multiple blockchains. In this exchange, we use the Tron network (TRC-20) because it has the lowest transfer fees (usually less than $1). You need a Tron-compatible wallet address to send or receive USDT. You can get one from TronLink, Trust Wallet, or use the deposit address from your exchange (Binance, KuCoin, etc.).
What happens if I don't pay in time?
After claiming an order, you have 2 hours to complete the USDT transfer and submit the TX hash. If you don't complete the payment in time, the order returns to "open" status and becomes available for other buyers. The seller's IOTAI remains safely in escrow. Orders themselves expire after 24 hours if no one claims them, and the IOTAI is returned to the seller.
Can I send USDT from Binance or another exchange?
Yes, absolutely! You can send USDT from any exchange (Binance, KuCoin, Bybit, OKX, etc.) or any personal wallet. Exchanges use internal pool wallets, so the on-chain sender address will be different from your account — but our system doesn't check the sender, only the destination and amount. Just make sure to:

• Select TRC-20 (Tron) as the withdrawal network
• Paste the seller's Tron address as the destination
• Send the exact amount shown in the instructions
• Copy the TX hash from your withdrawal history and paste it in the confirmation
What is the minimum price per IOTAI?
The minimum price is $0.10 USDT per IOTAI. Sellers can set any price equal to or above this minimum.
